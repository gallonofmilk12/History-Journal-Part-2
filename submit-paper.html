Submission Guidelines

Editorial Requirements

Before submitting your paper, please ensure it meets the following requirements:

  • 1,500-5,000 words in length (excluding citations)
  • Original research on a historical topic
  • Follows Chicago Manual of Style for citations
  • Includes an abstract (200 words maximum)
  • PDF format only

For complete guidelines and submission rules, please visit our About & Rules page.
